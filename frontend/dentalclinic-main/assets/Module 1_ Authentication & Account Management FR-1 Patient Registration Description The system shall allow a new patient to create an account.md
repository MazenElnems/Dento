Module 1: Authentication & Account Management FR-1 Patient Registration Description The system shall allow a new patient to create an account. Flow 1\. Patient opens the registration page. 2\. Patient enters: Full Name Email Address Mobile Number Password Confirm Password 3\. System validates the entered information. 4\. System creates a new patient account. 5\. Patient is redirected to the Medical History form. Business Rules Email must be unique. Mobile number must be unique. Password must satisfy security requirements. Medical History completion is required before booking. FR-2 Login Description The system shall allow registered users to log in. Supported Roles Patient Dentist Receptionist Admin Business Rules Invalid credentials shall display an error message. Users shall be redirected to their respective dashboards after successful login. Module 2: Medical History FR-3 Create Medical History Description The system shall allow patients to complete their medical history after registration. Information Collected Blood Pressure Issues Diabetes Heart Disease Allergies Current Medications Previous Surgeries Smoking Status Additional Notes Business Rules Medical History must be completed before booking appointments. Dentist shall be able to view Medical History. FR-4 Update Medical History Description The system shall allow patients to update their Medical History at any time. Business Rules Updated information shall be immediately available to dentists. System shall maintain modification timestamps. Module 3: Patient Dashboard FR-5 Dashboard Overview Description The system shall provide a dashboard containing: Quick Actions Upcoming Appointment Recent Notifications Recent Prescriptions Recent Dental Records FR-6 Quick Actions The system shall provide shortcuts to: Create Appointment View Appointments View Dental Records View Prescriptions Module 4: Appointment Management This is the core module. FR-7 Create Appointment Description The system shall allow patients to create an appointment. Flow 1\. Patient selects "Create Appointment". 2\. System displays available appointment slots for the next 4 weeks. 3\. Patient selects an available slot. 4\. System redirects patient to payment. 5\. Patient completes payment. 6\. Appointment is confirmed. Business Rules Patient does not choose a dentist. System automatically assigns an available dentist. Only available slots shall be displayed. Appointment is not confirmed until payment succeeds. If payment fails, appointment shall be cancelled automatically. FR-8 Appointment Slot Allocation Description The system shall manage dentist availability automatically. Business Rules First visit duration is 30 minutes. Multiple dentists may be available simultaneously. A time slot remains available while at least one dentist is available. A slot becomes unavailable only when all dentists are occupied. Confirmed appointments shall block the assigned time slot. FR-9 Appointment Cancellation Description Patients shall be able to cancel appointments. Business Rules Cancellation is allowed only up to 2 hours before appointment time. Cancellation requests made less than 2 hours before the appointment shall be rejected. Payment refunds are handled physically in the clinic. FR-10 Appointment Rescheduling Description Patients shall be be able to reschedule appointments. Business Rules Rescheduling is allowed only up to 4 hours before appointment time. System shall display available slots. Old slot shall be released after successful rescheduling. Supported Cases Phone booking Walk-in patient Patient without online account Business Rules Receptionist may create appointments for non-system patients. Receptionist shall provide: Patient Name Phone Number Appointment Time FR-13 Emergency Appointment Allocation Description Receptionists shall be able to allocate emergency appointments. Business Rules Existing appointments cannot be overridden. Only available slots may be assigned. Emergency appointments shall be marked as emergency cases. Information Stored Medication Name Dosage Frequency Duration Notes FR-20 Schedule Follow-Up Visits Description Dentists shall be able to schedule future visits.
